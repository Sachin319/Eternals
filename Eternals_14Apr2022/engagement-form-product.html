Common Questions

Are sustainable diamonds the same as mined diamonds? Are they real?

Sustainable diamonds have the same properties and characteristics as mined diamonds. Although they are created differently, sustainable diamonds are exactly the same as mined diamonds when it comes to quality. Sustainable diamonds have the advantage of offering more value at the same price.

Is buying sustainable diamonds more ethical than mined diamonds?

Mined diamonds are associated with unsafe work conditions, exploitative labor practices, and disreputable political factions. Meanwhile, sustainable diamonds are conflict-free products that are created in U.S. laboratories and have none of these associations.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy is 30 days after the date of purchase to addresses in Canada and the U.S.A. The property must be undamaged, and the customers must pay for shipping costs for the return. The product must be in proper condition without any wear and tear.

How are sustainable diamonds created?

Sustainable diamonds are created in a laboratory under special conditions using procedures called CVD. CVD stands for “Chemical Vapor Deposition.”

Is it “cheap” to buy sustainable diamonds?

Sustainable diamonds are real diamonds. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) considers sustainable and mined diamonds to be the same thing.

How can you see the difference between a sustainable diamond and a mined diamond?

Every sustainable diamond is marked with a tiny laser inscription and a lab report number on its girdle, the widest part of the diamond. These tiny marks can only be seen under high magnification.

What is your resizing policy?

Customers have one free resizing available up to one year after purchase. Rings must be in proper workable quality, and cannot have been previously resized. If your conditions don’t meet these terms, we are happy to offer resizing to you at an additional price. Please see our terms and conditions for more.
